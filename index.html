Recipes

All recipes are vegetarian, ≤ 20 minutes active time, and use only ingredients shown in your photos (water/ice not counted).

Tempeh & Kale–Mushroom Stir-Fry15 min • serves 1

Ingredients

  • Tempeh 200g (cubed)
  • Kale 2 handfuls (chopped)
  • Mushrooms 200g (sliced)
  • Minced garlic 1 tsp
  • Soy sauce 1 tbsp
  • Stock powder 1/2 tsp + 2 tbsp water

Method

  1. Pan mushrooms with a splash of water 3–4 min.
  2. Add tempeh; cook 4–5 min, turning.
  3. Add garlic 30 sec.
  4. Add kale + stock-water + soy; toss 2–3 min until wilted.
Tempeh Mince Tomato–Spinach Skillet with Cauli-Rice18 min • serves 1

Ingredients

  • Tempeh mince 250g
  • Diced tomatoes 1/2 can
  • Spinach 2 handfuls
  • Minced garlic 1 tsp
  • Pizza sauce 2 tbsp (or pesto 1 tbsp)
  • Cauliflower 1/2 head (grated to ‘rice’)

Method

  1. Grate cauliflower; microwave 3–4 min.
  2. Pan tempeh mince 4–5 min.
  3. Add garlic 30 sec.
  4. Add tomatoes + pizza sauce; simmer 5–6 min.
  5. Stir spinach 1–2 min. Serve over cauli-rice.
Garlic–Soy Tofu & Black Bean Bowl (with warm cabbage slaw)15 min • serves 1

Ingredients

  • Tofu 300g (cubed)
  • Black beans 1/2 can (rinsed)
  • Red cabbage 2 cups (sliced)
  • Minced garlic 1 tsp
  • Soy sauce 1 tbsp
  • Apple cider vinegar 1 tsp

Method

  1. Microwave tofu 90 sec; drain.
  2. Pan tofu 5–6 min (use splash water if needed).
  3. Add garlic 30 sec.
  4. Add beans + soy + 2 tbsp water; cook 2–3 min.
  5. Toss cabbage with vinegar + 1 tsp soy; warm 1 min. Serve.
Chickpea–Beet Crunch Salad with Cottage Cheese10 min • serves 1

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas 1/2 can (rinsed)
  • Beetroot 1/2 can (chopped)
  • Red cabbage 1–2 cups (sliced)
  • Cottage cheese 200g
  • Apple cider vinegar 1 tsp

Method

  1. Combine chickpeas, beetroot, cabbage.
  2. Stir through vinegar.
  3. Top with cottage cheese.
10‑Minute Lentil–Tomato–Spinach Tofu Stew12 min • serves 1

Ingredients

  • Lentils 1 can (rinsed)
  • Diced tomatoes 1/2 can
  • Tofu 200g (cubed)
  • Spinach 2 handfuls
  • Stock powder 1/2 tsp + 3/4 cup water
  • Minced garlic 1 tsp

Method

  1. Simmer stock-water; add garlic.
  2. Add lentils + tomatoes; simmer 5 min.
  3. Add tofu; simmer 3 min.
  4. Stir spinach 1–2 min.
Mango Jelly Protein ‘Cheesecake’ Cup7 min active • serves 1

Ingredients

  • High protein yoghurt 200g
  • Lite jelly 1 sachet (prepared)
  • Frozen mixed berries 100g
  • Protein powder 1/2 scoop (optional)

Method

  1. Make jelly with slightly less water; cool 2 min.
  2. Whisk yoghurt (+ optional protein).
  3. Layer berries + yoghurt; pour jelly over.
  4. Chill until set (1–2 hrs).
Microwave Chocolate Protein Brownie Mug6 min • serves 1

Ingredients

  • Chocolate protein 1 scoop
  • Greek yoghurt 120g
  • Cocoa (or shaved dark choc) 1 tsp
  • Baking powder 1/4 tsp

Method

  1. Mix to thick batter in a mug.
  2. Microwave 45–70 sec.
  3. Rest 1 min.
Banana Protein ‘Nice‑Cream’5 min • serves 1

Ingredients

  • Frozen banana 180g
  • High protein yoghurt 150g
  • Chocolate protein 1/2 scoop

Method

  1. Blend banana to crumbly then creamy.
  2. Add yoghurt + protein; blend 15–30 sec.
  3. Serve.
